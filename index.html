Improving your tennis is not easy. Tennis is a complex and difficult game, involving just about every movement the body is capable of making. And how you move is only half the story - tennis involves your mind too.

The Coaching philosophy

The coaching philosophy I use is based on a concept that includes:

1.Giving an appropriate level of attention to each player I work with

2.Integrated formation of each student from a personal and tennis perspective

3.Development of the basic principles of competition – stroke technique, footwork, strategy and tactics, mental and physical development Discipline, work ethic and effort

4.Professionalism = Commitment

5.In order to become a professional, a player must act like one - based on a belief in a strong work ethic, a humble attitude and good role models
